Aim

Create a personal organiser app. Within the app the main feature will be providing the weather forecast in your location. The other aspect is a built in planner where based on the weather, a list of suggested activities around the Wellington Region will be peovided, these can then be added to your planner.

Basically a weather app turned planner, everything in one place for all your needs to plan the adventures of your day/week.

Target Audience

Everyone checks the weather when making plans so Weatherly is for all. There are multiple weather apps around but Weatherly's built in planner makes life just that much painfree to plan, plus the simple layout and graphics of Weatherly appeals to users who will find it easy to understand and use.

Technical Construction

API for Weatherly will be sourced from 4 different sources:

Weather will use the DarkSky.net API.
Events will be sourced from the EventFinda API and The Movie DB API.
The Planner will be sourced from the Google Calender API.

Visual Direction

Colour Pallete, Logo, Font, Graphics

I decided to go with a quite vibrant colourscheme which i thought appropriate to the app idea. The colours are also cohesive and contrasting to be easy on the eye.

A simple yet elegant logo has been created to represent the Weatherly app.

I chose two different fonts Calibri and Rasa, which i will use in thier various styles to complement the stripped back symbols. Although they belong to different font families I find that their combination work well together.

I have kept the graphics very simplistic to go along with a minimalistic theme.
